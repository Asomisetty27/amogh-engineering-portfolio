THETA

GPU Thermal Forensics Platform

Redbrick VC Investment | June 2026

The Problem

  • GPU operators are flying blind on thermal degradation
  • Temperature alone is ambiguous (workload vs. cooling failure?)
  • Existing tools don't separate the two signals
  • Operators discover cooling failures when throttling happens — too late
  • Result: Production failures, SLA breaches, costly RMAs

The Insight

R_theta = (T_junction - T_ambient) / P_GPU
  • In healthy hardware: R_theta is stable (~0.72 C/W under load)
  • When cooling degrades: R_theta rises, even at constant power
  • This single metric from NVML telemetry detects failure before throttling
